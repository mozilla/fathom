Product Overview

The PVC-encased AFC Cable Systems Liquidtite 1/2 in. x 25 ft. Flexible Steel Conduit is designed to hold power, control and communications cables in dry, wet or oily locations.

  • Helps shield power, control and communications cables from heat, moisture, oil and sunlight
  • Rugged PVC jacket allows for burial in earth or for concrete encasement
  • Hot-dipped zinc, galvanized low-carbon steel core
  • Permanent circuit ground protection provided through a continuous copper bonding strip
  • UL- and CSA listed
  • Note: Product may vary by store.
