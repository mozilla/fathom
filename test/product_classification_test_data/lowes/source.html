      "Handle broke out of the ferrule on the FIRST day"

      Review Rating 1 Stars
      Reviewed on 08/30/2015
      Not Recommended

      All of my 10+ year old wooden-handled shovels have been looking a little weathered (surface cracks, splintering, etc), so I decided to treat myself to one of these fancy new fiberglass-handled units before digging out 3 small shrubs on the side of my house.

      While digging out the last of the shrubs, the handle bent noticeably out of the ferrule that connects it to the shovel end. I have to assume that the handle has snapped and cracked inside the shovel head, too.

      I finished digging out and removing the shrub with one of my old wooden shovels that I had intended to replace, and will be returning this broken fiberglass shovel right back to the store tomorrow for a refund.

      SteveC
