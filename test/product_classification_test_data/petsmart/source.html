Bali Breeze Limited Edition Mermaid Tail

  
  
In stunning detail, this MER-mazing swimmable fabric mermaid tail gradually goes from yellow to green to blue scales, making any mermaid shine as bright as the sun on land or in the water! Plus, the sparkle and shimmer of our Limited Edition custom printed swimsuit material makes this mermaid tail one of the hottest products on the market!

  
Lightweight, comfortable, and built-to-last, the Bali Breeze Limited Edition Mermaid Tail will knock your fins off with its gorgeous color combination and unique shiny texture. Get the unique, custom look of a silicone tail without the weight or unnecessary expense!

  
Care Instructions: Keeping your Fin Fun Limited Edition Mermaid Tail in great shape is easy! Thanks to our ultra-durable custom fabric, you can wash this tail in your washing machine on the gentle cycle in cool water. Please avoid high temperature water (hot tubs) and dryers which can damage the elastic fibers in your tail and cause your tail to lose its shape. To dry your tail after washing, simply hang, or lay your tail flat to dry.

REINFORCED TAIL TIPS WITH 60-DAY TAIL TIP WARRANTY

     
Fin Fun now has exclusive reinforced tail tips backed by our 60-Day Tail Tip Warranty! Our unique new patent-pending technology protects you from holes, tearing, and shredding that can often occur in the vulnerable tip area.

     
Any swimmable mermaid tail from our Mermaidens and Limited Edition lines featuring reinforced tips is eligible for one FREE tail replacement if you develop holes or tears in your tips within 60 days of purchase.  No other mermaid tail company offers a warranty as good as this! As Fin Fun continues to be the most innovative mermaid tail brand in the world, we can focus on providing the best products available to ensure our customers’ satisfaction.
